||MINI BANKING SYSTEM||

PROBLEM STATEMENT: Many have to work around managing multiple bank accounts, tracking deposit, withdrawal, and transfers manually. This is a simple project that can create a digital solution for users to manage accounts, transactions, and balances with ease.

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT:
Manage a number of customer accounts.
-Perform deposits, withdrawals, and transfers.
-Keep a record of transactions to ensure accountability.
-Generating reports for all accounts.

TARGET USERS:
- Students learning basic banking operations.
- Anyone looking to understand and simulate account management.
- Anyone looking for simple, easy-to-use banking management software.


HIGH LEVEL FEATURES
1. Create new accounts with unique account numbers.
2. Make deposits in accounts.
3. Withdraw cash from accounts.
4. Transfer money between accounts. 
5. Show account balances and details. 
6. Transaction history viewing.
7. Generate report of all accounts.
